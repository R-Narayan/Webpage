The Cozmo Bot

The cozmo bot is a prototype in quest to make a friendly robot. The bot uses AI and Image processing to detect and track human faces and has the ability to recognize human speech and act upon it. A Raspberry Pi controls various servos and other motors via an Arduino UNO for actuating gestures such as handshaking and neck movements.
