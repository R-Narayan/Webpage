Events

RoboWars

11th ,12th and 13th Oct 2019, Outdoor Stadium
This is an event based on combat robotics. There were three categories of bots- 15kg , 30kg and 60 kg. We had India’s largest and safest arenas. 40+ Robots participated in the competition. This included two international teams. The dance and the music club were also involved in the event. The prize pool was 2.5 L. It was the biggest event in Gravitas and the biggest robowars in South India. It was one of the biggest robowars in India.
